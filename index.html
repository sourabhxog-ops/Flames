What is the FLAMES Compatibility Test?

FLAMES is a nostalgic, classic game traditionally played with pen and paper by students and young couples to predict relationship outcomes. The acronym stands for: **Friends, Love, Affection, Marriage, Enemy, and Sibling**.

While primarily used as an entertaining game, it provides an amusing, structured algorithm that continues to be a popular social media trend for exploring relationship chemistry.
